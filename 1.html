Welcome to the D²S²: Discharge Decision Support System Demo. D²S² is a proprietary discharge decision support tool system that assembles key data upon admission into a scoring system and in turn is able to identify patients at high-risk who should be referred for post-acute services to prevent readmission. Because the tool is used at the beginning of a hospital stay, the tool can help discharge planners and care coordinators identify high-risk patients quickly, and provide them enough time to coordinate the right care for high-need patients post-discharge.
