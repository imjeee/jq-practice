To assess a patients cognition status, we recommend using the animal fluency test as a useful diagnostic tool. The animal fluency test requires the patient to name as many animals as possible within a 60-second time period. If they can name at least 15 animals within the 60-second time period, select Cognitively Intact below. If the patient cannot name at least 15 animals, select Cognitively Impaired.
